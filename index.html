About Me

I am Professor Auxiliar in Mathematics at Universidade Aberta and Integrated Member at NOVA Math. My research background is in the area of Universal Algebra and may be broadly described as studying algebraic structures to understand their complexity as well as their interaction with various non-algebraic subjects. These days, my interests are in Semigroup Theory, Group Theory, and Combinatorics.
